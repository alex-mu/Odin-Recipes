Cake Recipe

Oven baked cake made with love, and a little bit of structure from the kitchen

Ingredients
  • 3 cups Flour
  • 1 cup Sugar
  • 1 Tablespoon salt
  • Milk or Water to mix
  • Flavoring of personal preference
Directions
  1. Pre heat the oven to 170 degreees
  2. Add flour, sugar and salt and mix into a container
  3. Add milk/water to mixture anc beat until smooth and then add flovoring for taste
  4. Add mixture to container shape for the oven and put in oven for 30 minutes
  5. Enjoy your cake
